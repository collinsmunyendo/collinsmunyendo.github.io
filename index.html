Greetings! I am a Master student in Informatics at the University of California, Irvine, working with Dr. Anne Marie Piper. I work in HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) and Social Computing.

I am interested in inclusive design for people who are vulnerable or marginalized in physical or digital environments (e.g., older adults, d/Deaf people). I often take a socio-cultural lens to examine people's situated use of new technology.

I am also lucky to be advised by Dr. Xin Tong. Before coming to UCI, I was luckily led into the world of HCI by Dr. Xianghua Ding at Fudan University, where I found my passion for design for aging.
