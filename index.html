About

I am a 2nd year Master student at Department of Informatics, University of California, Irvine. I currently work with Dr. Anne Marie Piper. I feel lucky to be part of Accessibility Research Collective. I received my B.Phil. at Fudan University.

Research Interests

My research aims to make technology more inclusive to people who are under-represented in mainstream design, e.g., older adults, d/Deaf people. I am interested in design opportunities from people's everyday life and real-world settings, especially social contexts of people's technology use.
