I am a Ph.D student in the [Usable Security and Privacy Lab](https://gwusec.seas.gwu.edu/), School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, the [George Washington University](https://www.gwu.edu), advised by Prof. [Adam Aviv](https://adamaviv.com/). My research interests lie in usable security and privacy, particularly in underrepresented communities.

Prior to joining GW, I obtained my master's degree in Information Technology from [Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.cmu.edu/) focused on Cybersecurity and Software Engineering. I have a bachelor's degree in Information Technology from [Strathmore University](https://www.strathmore.edu).

In 2019, I was privileged to attend [HashiConf '19](https://hashiconf.com/) in Seattle, Washington and [Devcon V](https://archive.devcon.org/) in Osaka, Japan under full ride scholarships where I networked with leading technologists from around the globe leveraging different tools to make the world a better place.
