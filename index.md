I am an incoming Ph.D student at the [Usable Security and Privacy Research Group](http://gwusec.org), School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, [The George Washington University](https://www.gwu.edu), advised by Prof. [Adam Aviv](https://adamaviv.com/). My research interests lie in usable security and privacy, particularly in underrepresented communities.

Prior to joining GW, I obtained my master's degree in Information Technology from [Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.cmu.edu/). I have a bachelor's degree in Information Technology from [Strathmore University](https://www.strathmore.edu).

In 2019, I had the privilege of attending [HashiConf '19](https://hashiconf.com/) in Seattle, Washington and [Devcon '19](https://archive.devcon.org/) in Osaka, Japan under full scholarships. These conferences enabled me to network and learn from technologists around the world using different tools to make the world a better place.
